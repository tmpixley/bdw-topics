Understanding Float Collapse

Since I'm fairly new to front end development I was grateful to find this detailed article on Everything You Never Knew About CSS Floats. The author starts by detailing what floats are and how they work on a basic level. He explains how setting an element to float affects the bordering boxes of the elements involved, and what to do in order to get margins to work properly. .

He covers the basic rules that govern the position of a floated element and how floated elements of varying heights will be positioned as well as how right floated items appear in reverse order. Finally, he talks about how a parent containing only floated children will have a collapsed height and how you can solve it.
